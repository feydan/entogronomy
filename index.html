    ENTOGRONOMY

  • Let us explain how you can help.

    Did you just see a bug on your crop? Record which insect pests (or damage) you see on which crops to contribute to an interactive map and inform agricultural research. This map can allow you to communicate in real-time about the pests that are found in your area with your neighbors and community. You can use this map to track pest outbreaks and plan pest control strategies in advance. Local extension agents can rapidly be alerted to the intensity of pest problems in your area allowing more timely coordinated action to prevent yield loss to your crops. Agricultural researchers can then use this real-time information to more easily stay informed of the severity of the pest problems currently occurring and better serve you by planning projects focused on current needs. Just enter the name of the crop, the pest you suspect, your location, and a picture (optional) below!
